---
description: Planner Phase 2 · large ideas only — decompose the idea into epics with dependency edges.
---

As you begin this step, call `cyboflow_report_step` with `step_id` `epics`.

For a `large` idea, decompose it into epics with dependency edges, each epic linked
back to the originating idea via the `cyboflow_*` tools. A `small` idea skips epics
— go straight to `/cyboflow-tasks`. Presence of epics is the post-decomposition
signal that the idea was large.
