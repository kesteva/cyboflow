As you begin this step, call `cyboflow_report_step` with `step_id` `research`.

If the idea benefits from external context, pull in docs, prior art, and library
references (web search / context7). Fold the findings into the idea body via the
`cyboflow_*` tools. Skip this step when the idea is already well understood — it is
optional.
